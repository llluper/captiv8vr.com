Our Vision

Our goal is to make an accessible, interactive, virtual reality workspace to increase productivity, enhance work quality, and encourage group creativity & collaboration.

 

Work from anywhere, with anyone, in complete serenity and privacy => potentially increasing your productivity and creativity.

 

What is VR?

''Virtual Reality (VR) is the use of computer technology to create a simulated environment.'' VR places the user inside an experience. Instead of viewing a screen in front of them, users are immersed and able to interact with 3D worlds.

Menus, Controllers, Keyboards

We have just completed a prototype for our menu structures and have added it to our VR workspace. We have also been working on game/object controllers in which the user will be able to use to interact with objects by looking at them for a certain amount of time. So when do keyboards come into all of this? We now have the ability to type in the office environment. It is only a small step towards the end goal but it is a step none the less. We can now type in the VR world live. At the moment the text is just floating in space.. But it's there! The next challenge is trying to implement the ability to save the text to a document, and then look into text stylising and adding other features we want for our text editor.
